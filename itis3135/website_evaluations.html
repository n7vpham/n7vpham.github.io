Website Evaluations

Craiglist.org || BAD

A free-to-use website for viewing and posting local advertisements

  • Contrast: There is very little to no contrast as the website primarily uses a combination of bright colors. For example, sky blue on white.
  • Repetition: The website makes good use of repetition. Visual elements like color, font, font size, shape, and line thickness remain consistent throughout.
  • Alignment: Because everything is aligned in columns with no regard to anything else, the horizontal alignment is a mess.
  • Proximity: Poor alignment leads to a lot of empty space, making related elements appear separated.

Amazon.com || GOOD

The leading e-commerce website with net sales in the hundred billions

  • Contrast: Utilizing black and gray on white background color, the website creates the perfect contrast to make products stand out.
  • Repetition: All repeating elements are designed consistently, making the website look uniform and professional.
  • Alignment: Elements are lined up neatly, creating a sense of cohesion and unity.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped together, whether those, unrelated, are separated with white space. This design improves the shopping experience of customers by making everything clearer.
