Project Overview

Project Name: Charlotte Giants

  • Purpose: This website will serve as a recruting platform for the Charlotte Giants, a newly found team aiming to achieve the highest rank in an online game called Gigantic.
  • Intended users: For UNCC's students who like playing competitive games.
  • Overview of content: The website will explain what Gigantic is about, how to play the game, who the Charlotte Giants are, and why you should join the team. It will also include a knowledge quiz and a form for potential candidates to fill out.
