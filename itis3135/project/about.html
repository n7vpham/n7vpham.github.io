About

The Charlotte Giants team was founded by Tang Ye, a passionate gaming enthusiast and PHD student in nanoscale science. With a deep understanding of complex scientific principles, Tang brings analytical thinking and strategic insight to the gaming world. He is experienced in competitive gaming and has competed at various local tournaments. Tang's vision for the Charlotte Giants is to create a community where gamers can come together to enjoy Gigantic in a casual yet competitive environment.
